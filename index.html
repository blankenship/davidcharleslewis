In Loving Memory

David Charles Lewis

Nov 22, 1949 – May 12, 2020

David Charles Lewis was born November 22, 1949 in San Antonio, Texas, the firstborn child of Betty Jean Gossett and Loys Vernell Lewis. He was a 6th generation Texan, having ancestors who settled the territory with Stephen F. Austin and subsequently fought in the Battle of Bexar before the Alamo. David had a long, celebrated professional life, starting work in the produce department of __ grocery store under the watchful eye of future brother-in-law, Roy Guerra, “the best boss he ever had”. David began service as a San Antonio police officer and continuing on to become a San Antonio police officer, soldier for the National Guard, the youngest IRS agent.
